About the Event

This conference gathers young prespective researchers working at the forefront of quantum chemistry, electronic structure theory, and computational methods with single goal; to push the boundaries of current teoretical chemistry approaches. Topics include strongly correlated systems, excited states, density functional theory, and molecular dynamics with application on both biologic and artificial molecular and solid state systems.
